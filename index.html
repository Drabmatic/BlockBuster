Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 30, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how Block Buster ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Block Buster collects minimal information to provide and improve our services:

  • Leaderboard Data: When you submit a score to the global leaderboard, we collect your chosen display name and score. This information is stored using Firebase Realtime Database.
  • Device Information: Our advertising partners may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads.
  • Analytics: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to understand how players interact with the game and to improve the experience.

Advertising

Block Buster displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data to personalize ads. You can learn more about Google's data practices at Google Privacy Policy.

You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings or by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements
  • Firebase Realtime Database: For storing global leaderboard scores

These services have their own privacy policies governing the use of your information.

Data Retention

Leaderboard scores are retained indefinitely unless you request deletion. Advertising data is managed according to Google's retention policies.

Children's Privacy

Block Buster is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your leaderboard data by contacting us at the email below. You can also reset your advertising identifier through your device settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
